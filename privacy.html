Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 June 2026

Your trust matters to me. This explains what I collect, why, and how I keep it safe. In short: I only use your details to look after you and your orders, and I never sell or share them.

1. What I collect

  • Your name, email address and phone number.
  • Occasion details, the people in your Occasion Book, and important dates.
  • Order history, flavour preferences, and any cake photos you share with me.

2. Why I collect it

  • To prepare and fulfil your orders.
  • To run my personal occasion reminder service, so I can reach out before the dates you care about.
  • To reply to your enquiries and keep in touch about your orders.

3. Sharing

  • Never sold: Your information is never sold or shared with any third party for marketing.
  • Service providers: I use trusted tools to send email and store data securely. They only process your information so I can run this service, never for their own use.

4. How long I keep it

  • Your information is held for as long as you remain an active customer or stay in my Occasion Book.
  • You may ask me to delete it at any time.

5. Your choices and rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of the information I hold about you at any time.
  • Deletion: Email hello@hazelscakelounge.co.za to have your details removed.
  • Messaging: You can opt out of email or WhatsApp reminders at any time. See my Messaging Terms.
  • Consent: Agreeing to reminders is never a condition of placing an order.

6. Questions

  • If you have any questions about your privacy, please email me at hello@hazelscakelounge.co.za.
