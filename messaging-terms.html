Messaging & privacy

Messaging Terms

Effective date: 23 June 2026

1. WhatsApp messaging terms

  • Sender: Hazel's Cake Lounge.
  • Message types: Personal occasion reminders sent directly by Hazel. These are not automated bulk messages.
  • Frequency: A maximum of two WhatsApp messages per occasion per year.
  • Opt out: Reply STOP at any time. Your opt out is immediate and permanent until you choose to opt back in.
  • Rates: Standard carrier rates may apply.
  • Consent: Your consent to receive WhatsApp messages is never a condition of placing an order.

2. Email communication terms

  • Types: Enquiry confirmations, occasion reminders, Cake Memory Cards, order updates, and occasional personal notes from Hazel.
  • Unsubscribe: Reply UNSUBSCRIBE to any email at any time.

3. Data and privacy

  • What is collected: Name, email, phone number, occasion details, order history, flavour preferences, and cake photos.
  • Why: To provide a personalised occasion reminder service and to fulfil orders.
  • Sharing: Your data is never sold or shared with any third party under any circumstances.
  • Retention: Your data is held for as long as you remain an active customer. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Deletion requests: hello@hazelscakelounge.co.za
  • Right to access: You may request a copy of all data held about you at any time.
