The Occasion Book

Every occasion. Remembered.

Every time you order with me, I add your occasion to my personal record. Before each one arrives I will reach out so you never have to think about it. No reminders on your phone. No calendar alerts. I remember for you.

How I reach out

One month before A first, gentle word so there is time to plan.
Two weeks before A quiet check in so the details can settle.
One week before A final reminder, and the cake is taken care of.

Then every year, automatically. You never have to remember.

Who is the cake for?

What this does

Fill in the people and dates you never want to miss. I save each one in my Occasion Book and reach out before the day arrives, so the cake is sorted long before you would have remembered to ask. You are not ordering anything right now. You are simply making sure I remember for you.

Everyone is welcome

You do not need to have ordered before, and you do not need to be ordering today. Add the occasions you care about and I will keep them safe.

Yearly or one off

Birthdays and anniversaries I remember every single year. One off occasions like a wedding or a graduation I will not repeat, but you can still use this page to plan one for a date down the line. Add one person or your whole circle in one go.
