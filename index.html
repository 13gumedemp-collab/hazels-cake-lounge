02

A small kitchen,
an unhurried hand.

Hazel's began at a single marble bench, with the conviction that the best things are made in small numbers and given time. We bake to order, never to a shelf — so what arrives at your table was made for it.

Seasonal fruit from growers we know by name. Chocolate and vanilla chosen for character over convenience. Finishes kept deliberately spare, so the flavour speaks first.
