The fine print

Terms & Conditions

Effective date: 25 June 2026

These terms cover ordering with Hazel's Cake Lounge. By placing an order you agree to them. I keep everything personal and clear, so if anything is ever unsure, just ask me.

1. Orders and lead time

  • Made to order: Every bake is made fresh for you, in the flavour, size and finish we agree on.
  • Notice: Please allow at least 4 days' notice so each bake is fresh for your date. Larger or more detailed orders may need more time.
  • Confirmation: An enquiry is not a confirmed order. Your order is confirmed once details are agreed and payment is received.

2. Payment

  • Full payment upfront: Full payment is required to confirm and secure your order and your date.
  • Securing your date: Dates are only held once payment has been received. Until then, the date remains open to others.

3. No refunds

  • All sales are final: Once an order is confirmed and paid, it is non-refundable. This is because each bake is made to order especially for you and cannot be resold.
  • If something is wrong: If you are ever unhappy with your order, please contact me as soon as possible on the day so I can make it right personally.

4. Changes and cancellations

  • Changes: I will always try to accommodate changes, but they are subject to availability and the time left before your date. Once baking has begun, changes may not be possible.
  • Cancellations: As orders are paid in full upfront and made especially for you, cancellations are non-refundable.

5. Collection and delivery

  • Collection: Orders can be collected at an agreed time and place.
  • Delivery: Delivery is available within a local area for a fee, arranged in advance. Once an order has been collected or delivered and signed for, responsibility for it passes to you.

6. Looking after your cake

  • Freshness: Cakes are best enjoyed on the day. I will share simple storage notes with your order where helpful.
  • Handling: Tiered and decorated cakes need care when moving and storing. I am happy to advise.

7. Messaging and privacy

  • How I send WhatsApp and email messages is set out in my Messaging Terms.
  • How your information is handled is set out in my Privacy Policy.

8. Questions

  • If anything here is unclear, please email me at hello@hazelscakelounge.co.za and I will gladly explain.
